MD905 Update Log

MD905 is a device that can verify the authenticity of Apple Lightning cables. Unlike other instruments on the market that only test MFi cables, this device takes into account the complexity of charging port updates. It integrates USB-A, USB-C, MicroUSB, and Lightning ports, providing high-precision MFi testing, as well as voltage, current, and transformer testing.
